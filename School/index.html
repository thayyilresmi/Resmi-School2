St. John’s High School

About Us

St. John’s High School, Delhi is known for its academic excellence, sports facilities and co-curricular activities. It is one of the best school in Delhi. Our St. John’s High School in Delhi, is powered by a spacious Library, wonder rooms, music & dance rooms, English lab, Math lab and Science labs. The use of technology for teaching and learning makes the learning process most enriching and effective in Edify School, one of the best Private Schools in Delhi.

The St. John’s High School, Delhi, is a co-educational school. It was established in the year 1991. It’s affiliated to CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) and the AFFILIATION Number is 1930139. The school is committed to providing world-class value-based education. The school is basically caters to day scholars but also admits residential.

The school is open to both boarders as well as day scholars from Standard 1 to Standard 12. The medium of instruction is English. The school campus is aesthetically planned as a secure, comfortable and convenient facility, providing students with an inspiring and motivating environment. The boarding facilities are like a home away from home, offering all comforts.
